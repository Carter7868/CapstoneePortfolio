Looking Ahead

Career Aspirations

I want to build things that fly, drive, and think. My goal is to work in drones and robotics — whether that means designing flight controllers, engineering autonomous navigation systems, or building the power systems that keep UAVs airborne longer.

I'm also deeply interested in PCB design and battery engineering. The intersection of compact electronics, high-efficiency power delivery, and real-world ruggedization is where I feel most at home as an engineer. I want to contribute to teams pushing what's possible in these areas — especially in applications with real-world impact like search and rescue, environmental monitoring, and autonomous systems.
